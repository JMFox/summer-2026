Oslo

Jun 27–28 · 2 Nights · Gateway to the Nordic Arc
Midnight sun on the Opera House rooftop. Floating sauna on the Oslofjord. Panoramic hike above the city. Two days before the Bergen Railway carries you west.

The Thief

8.8 Landgangen 1, Tjuvholmen · ~114 rooms
Art hotel built around the Astrup Fearnley Museum on the Tjuvholmen waterfront. Every room has original contemporary art. Rooftop bar with fjord views.

Practical

  • Airport → City: Flytoget airport express, 19 min, runs every 10 min. ~NOK 220/pp.
  • Hotel → Oslo S: Taxi ~15 min, or tram. For Bergen Railway departure Day 3.
  • Bergen Railway: Departs Oslo S at 8:25 AM Jun 29. Arrives Bergen 2:30 PM. Book window seat, sit RIGHT side.
  • Getting around: T-bane (metro) + tram covers everything. Ruter app for tickets.
  • Currency: NOK. Cards accepted everywhere, no cash needed.
  • Sunset Jun 27: 11:30 PM. Twilight never ends — it stays light all night.
