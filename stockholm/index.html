The Fika Guide

Fika is a mandatory Swedish ritual: slow coffee, one perfect pastry, actual conversation. Not optional.

  • Vete-Katten — Since 1928. Old-school konditori. Princess cake.
  • Café Saturnus — Giant cardamom buns the size of your head.
  • Fabrique — Royal Court purveyor. Sourdough kanelbullar.
  • Café Pascal — Specialty third-wave coffee. Modern fika.
