Opus XVI ⭐

Bergen · 3 nights
Booking.com 9.1/10 · 65 rooms · Michelin Key · 1876 heritage bank
Converted from the 1876 Bergen Credit Bank into a Michelin Key hotel. Named after Edvard Grieg's Opus 16 (the Grieg family connection). Palatial lobby. Steps from Bryggen and the Fish Market.
