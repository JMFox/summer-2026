The Midnight Sun Effect

You're traveling during the longest days of the Nordic year. The sun sets after 11 PM and rises before 4 AM. There is no true darkness. Hike at midnight, eat dinner outside at 10 PM, watch golden light paint fjord walls at 2 AM. This is not a gimmick — it is a completely different relationship with time.
