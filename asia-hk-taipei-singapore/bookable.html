Hong Kong

#1

Bioluminescent Night Kayak — Cheung Chau Island

Klook / Hello! Hong Kong / Thrillophilia

Kayak under the stars along Cheung Chau's coastline. On moonless summer nights, bioluminescent algae glow blue-green beside your paddle as you stroke through the water. Late June/early July is peak season.

~5 hours~HK$700 (~US$90)Cheung Chau Windsurfing Centre
This is the closest thing to your Australian glow worm experience. Book around the new moon (July 7, 2026) for darkest skies.

Book on Klook

#4

Wing Chun Kung Fu with Master Sam Lau

Discover Hong Kong / Yip Man Martial Arts Association

Study basic Wing Chun at the Yip Man Martial Arts Association with Master Sam Lau — a first-generation student of Grandmaster Yip Man (Bruce Lee's own teacher). Then visit the Bruce Lee exhibition and statue.

Half dayHK$700/person (~US$90)Min 8 pax, private available
You are learning from someone who studied directly under Bruce Lee's master. The lineage is documented and irreplaceable.

Book via Discover HK

#5

Neon Signs Disappearing Heritage Photo Walk

Airbnb Experiences

A local photographer guides you through neon-drenched streets of Jordan and Yau Ma Tei, teaching photography techniques while sharing the history of HK's rapidly disappearing neon culture. Stops include iconic signs, rooftop views from Yau Ma Tei Car Park 8/F, Temple Street, and a Michelin noodle shop.

~2-3 hoursEveningJordan/Yau Ma Tei
Hong Kong's neon signs are disappearing rapidly. This is a time-sensitive cultural experience — within a few years, many will be gone. You leave with stunning images.

Book on Airbnb • Also: NEONSIGNS.HK open-top bus tours

Taipei / Taiwan

#3

River Tracing / Canyoning at Wulai

Klook

Natural water slides, cliff jumps, swimming through emerald canyons surrounded by jungle. The Jiajiuliao River in Wulai has gentle terrain perfect for first-timers, with a famous natural waterslide as the highlight. Professional coaches, all equipment provided. July = peak season, warm water.

~4-5 hoursWulai, New TaipeiBeginner-friendly
River tracing is uniquely Taiwanese — you won't find this sport organized the same way anywhere else. Sliding down natural rock chutes in a jungle canyon is genuinely magical.

Book on Klook

Full dayYilan (2 hrs from Taipei)Real community, not a theme park
Living cultural experience with an actual indigenous community. The Atayal have a fascinating facial tattoo tradition and rich textile heritage.

Singapore

#2

LED Glow Night Kayak through Mangroves — Pulau Ubin

GetYourGuide / Fever.sg

Singapore's first glow kayak tour. Paddle through mangrove forests at night in kayaks with customizable LED lights. Route goes from Pasir Ris through the Api Api River to Pulau Ubin. Fish flash beneath your lit kayak, bats swoop overhead, owls call from the trees, fireflies sparkle through the mangroves.

~2h 15minS$54.90 (~US$41)Max 15 travelers
Paddling through dark mangrove channels with your kayak glowing beneath you while nocturnal wildlife surrounds you. The Api Api River is a hidden gem most tourists never see.

Book on GetYourGuide • Book on Fever.sg
