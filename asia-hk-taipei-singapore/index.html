9:00 PM — Late Night Snack

Hot Star Large Fried Chicken (豪大大雞排)

The iconic Taiwanese XXL fried chicken cutlet — bigger than your face, pounded thin, marinated in five-spice and garlic, double-fried until shattering crispy. Hot Star started it all (original near Shilin), but stalls at every night market do their own version. Get it at Raohe before leaving. Dust with chili powder and white pepper. Taiwan's #1 street food and it is NOT the same as any fried chicken you've had.

Snack
★ Taiwan's legendary XXL fried chicken — the crispiest, most five-spiced bird on earth
