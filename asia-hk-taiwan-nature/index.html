Why June 26 – July 10

Every day of this window has a reason to exist

🐋

Peak Whale Watching Season

July is prime time off Hualien — sperm whales, spinner dolphins, and bottlenose pods. Turumoan Whale Watching Company (est. 1998) runs 6:30 AM departures. Nothing like this on the US East Coast.

🎆

HKSAR Eve Fireworks — June 30

Hong Kong's biggest annual fireworks show, Victoria Harbour. Caught perfectly on Night 4 with the 5-night HK plan. Watch from the Rosewood rooftop or harbourfront promenade.

☁️

Alishan Cloud Sea at Sunrise

Summer mornings regularly produce the mythic "sea of clouds" phenomenon at 2,200m elevation. Sun rises at 5:15 AM in July. Ancient cypress forest scent. No crowds in July vs. spring cherry season.

🌿

Lychee + Mango Peak (HK & Taiwan)

Peak lychee season in Hong Kong wet markets. Peak Irwin and Tainan mango in Taiwan — the best mangoes you will eat in your life. Sun Moon Lake new-harvest black tea also peaks in June-July.

🎇

Penghu Fireworks Festival (Bonus)

Running May–Aug 25, 2026 with Tuesday shows from Jun 30. Dragon Ball Z theme + drone light show. A 50-min flight detour from Taipei could catch a Tuesday show if you have flex time.

♨️

Villa 32 Hot Springs Finale

Beitou's dual-sulfur springs (white and green) are available year-round, but the contrast of coming down from Alishan's mountain cool straight into Villa 32's geothermal pools is uniquely summer.

Turumoan Whale Watching — Hualien Harbour

Alishan House

Alishan National Scenic Area, 2,200m elevation

Historic mountain lodge built in the Japanese colonial era. Inside the park, walking distance to the Zhushan Sunrise Train. The accommodation is functional not luxurious — the sunrise and ancient forest are worth the trade-off completely.

The alarm at 4:30 AM is the best alarm of the trip.

1 Night

Villa 32, Beitou

Relais & Châteaux, Beitou, Taipei

5 rooms only. Dual-sulfur hot springs (white sulfur + green sulfur, two different geological springs on one property). Private outdoor soaking pools for each room. The rarest hot spring experience in Taiwan. Book 3 months ahead minimum.

The trip's perfect final night: descend from mountains, soak in volcanic water, fly home tomorrow.

Ita Thao Aboriginal Village — Thao Breakfast

The Thao people are Taiwan's smallest indigenous group (about 800 people) and the only lake-dwelling tribe. At Ita Thao pier, eat breakfast from Thao food vendors: mochi wrapped in wild herb leaves, millet rice wrapped in taro, braised venison. Open 7 AM. A breakfast that exists nowhere else in the world.

Morning mist is common in July at 750m elevation — the lake's surface sometimes disappears entirely into soft white fog, with temple rooflines and pagoda tips poking through. Board the lake boat between Shuishe, Ita Thao, and Xuanguang piers. One of the most ethereal natural sights in Taiwan.
